Rules

Matches are standard best of 5 games. (11 points per game, win by 2).

The Open Division will play 4 rounds of Swiss, and the Senior Division will play a round robin.


Each round consists of 6 matches total, with 2 matches played on each of the 3 courts.

Generally, the top 2 Open matches will be played on court 1, the bottom 2 Open matches will be played on court 2, and the Senior matches will be played on court 3.

The players who will be playing on the same court within a round, are expected to referee when not playing.

Rules

The Open Division will play 5 rounds of Swiss.
The Senior Division will play a Round Robin (Each player plays every other player once).

After 5 rounds, the 2 players with the best records in each division will play a championship match.

Matches are standard best of 3 games. Championship matches are best of 5.

Games are 11 points per game, win by 2, with 90 seconds rest between games.

Each round consists of 6 matches total, with 2 matches played on each of the 3 courts.
The top 2 Open matches will be played on court 1, the bottom 2 Open matches will be played on court 2, and the Senior matches will be played on court 3.

The players who will be playing on the same court within a round, are expected to referee when not playing.
